Alyssa Lee

Striving for inclusivity in all my designs...

A designer passionate about inclusivity and accessibility while creating intuitive experiences that are aesthetically pleasing.

My background as a Speech-Language Therapist has inspired me to create experiences that are inclusive and accessible for those with communication disabilities. This has allowed me to obtain skills in:

  • Clear written and verbal communication
  • Using Figma to create wireframes, user flows, and mockups of apps and websites
  • Developing websites using HTML, CSS, and Javascript

In my spare time I also like to expand my horizons by illustrating, hiking, and trying new foods.
